# 📊 Business Performance & Profitability Analysis for an E-Commerce Retailer

## 🎯 Project Overview

This project analyzes 9,994 retail transactions from an e-commerce business to identify revenue drivers, profitability trends, customer behavior, discount effectiveness, and geographic performance.

The analysis transforms raw transaction data into actionable business insights that support pricing decisions, inventory planning, customer retention, and profitability optimization.

---

## 🚀 Business Problem

The business wanted to understand:

* Which products generate the highest revenue and profit
* Which products and regions are causing losses
* How discounts impact profitability
* Which customer segments create the most value
* Which markets should receive future investment

The objective was to identify opportunities to improve profitability and support data-driven decision-making.

## 📈 Key Findings

### Revenue Performance

* November generated the highest sales revenue.
* Technology was the highest revenue-generating category.
* Phones and Chairs were the strongest revenue-driving sub-categories.

### Profitability Performance

* December generated the highest profit.
* Technology achieved the highest profit margin (15.61%).
* Furniture generated strong sales but maintained the lowest profit margin (3.88%).

### Discount Analysis

* Discounts above 30% consistently resulted in negative profits.
* A 50% discount generated the highest sales value but also produced the largest average loss.

### Customer Analysis

* Consumer customers generated the highest sales and profit.
* Home Office customers demonstrated the best sales-to-profit efficiency.

### Geographic Analysis

* West was the highest-performing region in terms of sales, profit, and profit margin.
* California and New York were the most profitable states.
* Texas was the largest loss-making state.

### Root Cause Analysis

A detailed investigation of Texas revealed:

* Office Supplies and Furniture were the primary loss-generating categories.
* Binders were the largest contributor to losses.
* Additional losses came from Appliances, Furnishings, Machines, Chairs, Bookcases, and Tables.

---

## 💡 Strategic Recommendations

1. Increase investment in Technology products due to their strong revenue contribution, profitability, and profit margin performance.

2. Review pricing and discount strategies for Furniture products, particularly Tables and Bookcases.

3. Limit discounts above 30% because higher discount levels consistently result in negative profits.

4. Expand successful sales and marketing practices from the West region into lower-performing markets.

5. Conduct detailed profitability reviews in Texas, focusing on Office Supplies, Furniture, and Binders.

6. Prioritize customer retention initiatives for Consumer customers while identifying opportunities to grow the Home Office segment.

7. Implement profitability monitoring at the category, sub-category, and regional levels.
